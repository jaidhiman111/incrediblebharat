Welcome to Andaman & Nicobars

Andaman and Nicobar Islands are a group of islands located in the Bay of Bengal, known for their stunning beaches, crystal-clear waters, and rich biodiversity. The islands are a popular tourist destination, offering a range of activities such as snorkeling, scuba diving, and trekking. The capital city is Port Blair, which serves as the gateway to the islands.

Top Travel Destinations

Radhanagar Beach

One of Asia's best beaches with powdery white sand and turquoise waters.

Cellular Jail

Historical monument with a light & sound show on India's freedom struggle.

Elephant Beach

Snorkeling and water activities paradise on Havelock Island.
